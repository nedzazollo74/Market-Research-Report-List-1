<p><p>The packaging and paper adhesives market is categorized into three main types: water-based adhesives, solvent-based adhesives, and hot melt adhesives. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly and suitable for paper products, offering easy cleanup. Solvent-based adhesives provide strong bonding for challenging materials but can release volatile organic compounds. Hot melt adhesives are thermoplastic and provide quick bonding with excellent tack, making them ideal for fast production lines. Each type serves specific needs in packaging and paper applications, balancing performance and environmental considerations.</p></p>
