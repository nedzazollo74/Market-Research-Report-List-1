<p><ul><li>Open Cell</li><li>Closed Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVC foam sheets in the structural composites market are categorized into two primary types: open cell and closed cell. Open cell PVC foam is characterized by a network of interconnected pores, making it lightweight and flexible, suitable for applications requiring insulation and sound absorption. In contrast, closed cell PVC foam features tightly packed cells that are impermeable to water and offer greater durability and rigidity, ideal for structural support and applications demanding high strength and moisture resistance.</p></p>
