<p><p>Cable fault locating devices are essential tools used to detect and pinpoint faults in underground cable systems. The market includes various types, such as Cable Sheath Fault Locators, which identify issues in the cable's outer layer; Cable Route Tracers, used for mapping cable paths; Time Domain Reflectometers, which analyze reflections in signals to identify faults; Pin Pointers, designed for precise location of faults; and Voltage Surge Generators, which apply high voltage to detect insulation defects. Each type serves a specific purpose in maintaining cable integrity.</p></p>
