<p><p>The Alzheimer's Disease market comprises various treatment options, primarily focusing on Donepezil, Memantine, and Rivastigmine. Donepezil is a cholinesterase inhibitor that enhances cognitive function by increasing acetylcholine levels. Rivastigmine also functions similarly, targeting both acetylcholinesterase and butyrylcholinesterase enzymes. Memantine, on the other hand, is an NMDA receptor antagonist that helps regulate glutamate activity, protecting neurons from excitotoxicity. These medications aim to alleviate symptoms and improve the quality of life for patients, shaping a diverse and evolving market landscape.</p></p>
