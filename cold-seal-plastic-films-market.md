<p><p>Cold seal plastic films are specialized packaging materials utilized primarily in food and pharmaceutical industries. They are designed to adhere to themselves and other surfaces without the need for heat, making them ideal for packaging delicate products that could be damaged by high temperatures. Emerging trends include the development of biodegradable and recyclable cold seal films, aligning with sustainability initiatives in packaging practices.
