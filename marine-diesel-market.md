<p><strong>The Marine Diesel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Light Diesel Oil</li><li>Heavy-Duty Diesel Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine diesel market primarily consists of two main types: Light Diesel Oil (LDO) and Heavy-Duty Diesel Oil (HDDO). LDO is typically used in smaller vessels and for lighter applications due to its higher volatility and lower viscosity, providing quick start-up and efficient combustion. In contrast, HDDO is formulated for larger vessels and heavy machinery, offering better lubrication and improved performance under high loads. Both types cater to different operational needs, reflecting the diverse requirements of the maritime industry.</p></p>
