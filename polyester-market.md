<p><ul><li>Saturated Polyesters</li><li>Unsaturated Polyesters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyester market is primarily divided into two types: saturated and unsaturated polyesters. Saturated polyesters, characterized by their lack of double bonds, are typically used in textiles, films, and coatings due to their durability and resistance to environmental factors. Unsaturated polyesters, on the other hand, contain reactive double bonds and are often employed in resin applications like composite materials and fiberglass. Both types serve distinct purposes in various industries, demonstrating the versatility and importance of polyester in manufacturing.</p></p>
