<p><p>The Polyethersulfone Dialyzers market is primarily categorized into high permeability and low permeability types. High permeability dialyzers facilitate efficient solute clearance, offering faster dialysis sessions by allowing larger molecules to pass through their membranes easily. In contrast, low permeability dialyzers provide a finer filtration, making them more suitable for patients requiring slower, more controlled removal of substances. The choice between these types is based on patient needs, clinical requirements, and desired treatment outcomes in renal care.</p></p>
