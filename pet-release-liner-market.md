<p><p>The PET release liner market encompasses three primary types: silicone-coated, non-silicone coated, and no coating. Silicone-coated release liners are favored for their superior release properties, making them ideal for high-performance applications like medical and adhesive labels. Non-silicone coated liners offer a cost-effective alternative for less demanding applications, providing adequate performance for general use. The no coating market serves applications where adhesion is minimal, allowing direct contact with the substrate, often used in simpler packaging solutions.</p></p>
