<p><p>The Totally Implantable Access Port (TIAP) market includes two main types: titanium ports and plastic ports. Titanium ports are favored for their durability and biocompatibility, making them suitable for long-term use in patients requiring frequent vascular access. In contrast, plastic ports are generally lighter, cost-effective, and provide an alternative for patients who may not require extended access. Both types serve critical roles in oncology and other medical fields, facilitating chemotherapy, nutrition, and medication delivery efficiently.</p></p>
