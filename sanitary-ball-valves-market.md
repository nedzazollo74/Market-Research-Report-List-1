<p><ul><li>Clamp Connections</li><li>Weld Connections</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sanitary ball valves market is categorized by connection types, primarily comprising clamp connections and weld connections. Clamp connections allow for easy installation and maintenance, making them ideal for applications requiring frequent disassembly. These are commonly used in food and beverage, pharmaceutical, and biotechnology industries. In contrast, weld connections offer a permanent, robust solution ensuring a leak-proof seal, suitable for high-pressure and harsh environments. Both types are crucial for maintaining sanitary conditions in processing systems.</p></p>
