<p><p>Spading machines are categorized into PTO-driven and walk-behind types, each suited for different applications. PTO-driven spading machines attach to tractors, leveraging the power take-off to efficiently till large areas, making them ideal for commercial farming. Walk-behind spading machines, on the other hand, are more compact and user-friendly, designed for smaller plots or residential use. These offer greater maneuverability, allowing gardeners and landscapers to navigate tight spaces while providing effective soil aeration and preparation.</p></p>
