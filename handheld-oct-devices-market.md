<p><strong>The Handheld OCT Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spectral Domain OCT</li><li>Scanning Source OCT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld OCT devices include two main types: Spectral Domain Optical Coherence Tomography (SD-OCT) and Scanning Source Optical Coherence Tomography (SS-OCT). SD-OCT uses a broadband light source and interferometry to provide high-resolution cross-sectional images of tissues, commonly utilized in ophthalmology. SS-OCT, on the other hand, employs a tunable laser source for enhanced imaging depth and speed, allowing for dynamic assessments in various clinical scenarios. Both technologies enhance diagnostic capabilities in portable formats, improving accessibility and patient care.</p></p>
