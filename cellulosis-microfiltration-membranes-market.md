<p><p>The Cellulosis Microfiltration Membranes market encompasses two primary types: Cross Flow and Direct Flow. Cross Flow membranes operate by maintaining a constant flow of feed solution along the membrane surface, minimizing fouling and enhancing filtration efficiency. In contrast, Direct Flow membranes allow the feed solution to flow directly through the membrane, providing a simple design but potentially higher fouling rates. Both types are utilized in various applications, including water treatment, food processing, and biotechnology, catering to specific separation and filtration needs.</p></p>
