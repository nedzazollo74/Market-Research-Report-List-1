<p><ul><li>Optical</li><li>Electromagnetic (EM)</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental surgical navigation systems enhance precision during dental procedures by utilizing advanced tracking technologies. Optical systems rely on cameras and markers to provide visual feedback on the instruments' position. Electromagnetic (EM) systems use magnetic fields to track the location of instruments in real-time, offering high flexibility and ease of use. Hybrid systems combine both optical and EM technologies to leverage the strengths of each, resulting in improved accuracy and versatility for complex dental surgeries, ensuring optimal patient outcomes.</p></p>
