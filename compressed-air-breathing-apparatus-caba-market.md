<p><strong>The Compressed Air Breathing Apparatus (CABA) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Circuit CABA</li><li>Closed-Circuit CABA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Compressed Air Breathing Apparatus (CABA) market consists of two primary types: Open-Circuit CABA and Closed-Circuit CABA. Open-Circuit CABA releases exhaled air into the environment, providing a continuous supply of fresh air from a tank, ideal for short-duration tasks. In contrast, Closed-Circuit CABA recycles exhaled air by removing carbon dioxide, allowing for extended use in enclosed environments. Both types are crucial for safety in hazardous situations, including firefighting, industrial work, and emergency response.</p></p>
