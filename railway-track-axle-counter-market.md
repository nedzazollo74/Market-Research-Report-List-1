<p><strong>The Railway Track Axle Counter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rail Side Mounting</li><li>On-rail Mounting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The railway track axle counter market is categorized into two main types: rail side mounting and on-rail mounting. Rail side mounting involves placing the axle detection system adjacent to the track, providing a streamlined approach for monitoring axle movements without interfering with rail operations. In contrast, on-rail mounting integrates the axle counter directly onto the rail itself, allowing for more precise measurements and reduced external influences. Both configurations aim to enhance safety and efficiency in railway operations by accurately counting train axles.</p></p>
