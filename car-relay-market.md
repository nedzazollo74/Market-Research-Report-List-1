<p><strong>The Car Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normally Open</li><li>Normally Closed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the car relay market, relays are categorized into Normally Open (NO) and Normally Closed (NC) types based on their default states. Normally Open relays allow current to flow only when activated, functioning as a switch that connects circuits when energized. Conversely, Normally Closed relays maintain current flow until activated, turning off the circuit when energized. These types serve different automotive applications, ensuring efficient control of various electrical components such as lights, motors, and safety systems.</p></p>
