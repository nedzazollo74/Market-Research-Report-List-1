<p><p>The zinc sputtering target market is divided into two primary types: rotary and non-rotatable targets. Rotary targets are cylindrical and can achieve uniform erosion over a wider surface area, enhancing material utilization and system efficiency. In contrast, non-rotatable targets are typically flat and are easier to handle, making them suitable for smaller systems. Both types serve various applications in industries like electronics and optics, influencing the choice based on specific deposition requirements and equipment compatibility.</p></p>
