<p><p>High translucency zirconia blocks are advanced dental materials used in restorative dentistry. Multilayer zirconia blocks feature varying shades and translucency throughout the block, enabling more natural appearances in dental restorations. These are ideal for anterior teeth, providing aesthetic results. Monolithic zirconia blocks, on the other hand, are homogeneously dense and highly durable, suitable for both anterior and posterior applications. They offer strength and reliability, making them a popular choice for crowns and bridges without compromising aesthetics.</p></p>
