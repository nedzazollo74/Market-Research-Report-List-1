<p><strong>The Diet Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soluble Diet Fiber</li><li>Insoluble Diet Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diet fiber market is primarily divided into two types: soluble and insoluble dietary fibers. Soluble dietary fiber dissolves in water and forms a gel-like substance, which aids in lowering cholesterol and regulating blood sugar levels. Common sources include oats, legumes, and fruits. Conversely, insoluble dietary fiber does not dissolve in water and promotes digestive health by adding bulk to stool and aiding regularity. It is found in whole grains, nuts, and vegetables. Both types play crucial roles in overall health and wellness.</p></p>
