<p><p>The Filtering Facepiece Respirators (FFRs) market is divided into two main types: those without valves and those with valves. FFRs without valves provide a tighter seal, effectively filtering out airborne particles without allowing exhaled air to escape, which is ideal for protecting others in close proximity. In contrast, FFRs with valves feature a one-way valve that allows for easier exhalation, reducing heat and moisture buildup for the wearer, making them more comfortable during prolonged use, especially in hot environments.</p></p>
