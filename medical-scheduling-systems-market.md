<p><p>Medical scheduling systems are classified into two primary types: web-based and installed systems. Web-based systems operate in the cloud, offering users easy access to scheduling tools from any device with internet connectivity. They typically provide real-time updates and scalable solutions, ideal for multi-location facilities. In contrast, installed systems are software applications installed directly on local servers or computers. While they may offer enhanced security and customization, these systems often require more maintenance and can limit accessibility compared to their web-based counterparts.</p></p>
