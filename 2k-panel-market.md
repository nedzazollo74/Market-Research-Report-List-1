<p><p>The 2K panel market primarily features three types: IPS, VA, and others. IPS (In-Plane Switching) panels excel in color accuracy and wide viewing angles, making them ideal for professional tasks and multimedia. VA (Vertical Alignment) panels offer superior contrast and deep blacks, enhancing the viewing experience for movies and gaming. Other market types include TN (Twisted Nematic), known for fast response times but poorer color quality, and OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode), providing vibrant colors and deeper blacks at a higher cost.</p></p>
