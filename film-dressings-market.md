<p><p>The film dressings market is divided into two main types: traditional wound dressings and advanced wound dressings. Traditional wound dressings typically include simple adhesive films that provide a protective barrier against moisture and contaminants but lack advanced features. In contrast, advanced wound dressings incorporate sophisticated materials like hydrocolloids, hydrogels, and silicone, offering enhanced moisture control, infection management, and promoting faster healing. This differentiation addresses varying patient needs and wound types, influencing treatment outcomes and healthcare efficiency.</p></p>
