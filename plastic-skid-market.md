<p><p>The plastic skid market primarily includes two types: High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) and Polypropylene (PP). HDPE skids are known for their strength, durability, and resistance to chemicals, making them suitable for heavy loads and outdoor use. PP skids, on the other hand, are lighter and offer flexibility but maintain good chemical resistance. Both types are widely used in logistics and transport for storing and moving goods, providing cost-effective and eco-friendly solutions for various industries.</p></p>
