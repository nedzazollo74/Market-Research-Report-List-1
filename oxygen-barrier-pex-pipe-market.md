<p><p>The Oxygen Barrier PEX Pipe market is primarily categorized into three types: PE-Xa, PE-Xb, and PE-Xc. PE-Xa is produced through a chemical process known as peroxide cross-linking, offering superior flexibility and resistance to high temperatures. PE-Xb uses a silane cross-linking method, providing good durability and ease of installation. PE-Xc employs a physical method for cross-linking, offering cost advantages but with slightly lower flexibility. Each type caters to specific plumbing and heating applications, enhancing system efficiency and longevity.</p></p>
