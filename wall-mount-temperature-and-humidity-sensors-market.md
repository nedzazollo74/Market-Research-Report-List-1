<p><p>Wall mount temperature and humidity sensors can be categorized into three main types: capacitive, resistive, and other types. Capacitive sensors measure changes in electrical capacitance due to humidity variations, offering high accuracy and stability. Resistive sensors detect humidity through changes in electrical resistance, typically providing lower accuracy but at a lower cost. Other market types may include thermal or optical sensors, which employ different principles for measurement, catering to specific applications and environments in temperature and humidity monitoring.</p></p>
