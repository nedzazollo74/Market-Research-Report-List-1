<p><p>Rebound resilience testers are devices used to measure the elasticity and rebound properties of materials. The market is divided into two main types: manual and automatic rebound resilience testers. Manual testers require operators to perform tests by manually dropping a weight onto a sample and measuring the rebound. In contrast, automatic testers streamline the process with automated mechanisms for dropping the weight and recording results, ensuring greater consistency and efficiency in testing, making them suitable for high-throughput environments.</p></p>
