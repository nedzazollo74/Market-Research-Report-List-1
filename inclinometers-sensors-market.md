<p><p>Inclinometer sensors are critical for measuring angles of inclination and tilt. The market is primarily divided into two types: single-axis and dual-axis inclinometer sensors. Single-axis inclinometer sensors measure tilt along one plane, making them suitable for applications with limited directional requirements. In contrast, dual-axis inclinometer sensors measure tilt across two planes, offering enhanced versatility and precision for complex applications, such as in construction, geotechnical monitoring, and robotics. Both types are essential for ensuring stability and safety in various industries.</p></p>
