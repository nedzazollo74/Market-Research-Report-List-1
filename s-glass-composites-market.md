<p><strong>The S-Glass Composites Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Filament Winding</li><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Pultrusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The S-Glass composites market encompasses various manufacturing processes, including filament winding, injection molding, and pultrusion. Filament winding involves the precise winding of glass fibers around a mandrel, creating strong cylindrical structures ideal for tanks and pipes. Injection molding allows for the production of complex shapes by injecting a resin and fiber mixture into molds, facilitating mass production. Pultrusion, on the other hand, continuously draws fibers through a resin bath and then through a heated dying chamber, producing long, uniform profiles used in structural applications.</p></p>
